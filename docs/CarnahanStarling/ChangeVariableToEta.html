    Simplification of the η-Integrand — Eq.(6) #

    After the change of variable from ρ to η (Eq.5), the residual Helmholtz free energy integral has integrand

    $$\frac{4\eta - 2\eta^{2}}{(1-\eta)^{3}\,\eta}$$

    By factoring η from the numerator, 4η − 2η² = η(4 − 2η), and cancelling with the η in the denominator, the integrand simplifies to

    $$\frac{4 - 2\eta}{(1-\eta)^{3}}$$

    giving Eq.(6):

    $$\frac{A^{res}(ref)}{Nk_{B}T} = \int_{0}^{\eta}\!\frac{4 - 2\eta'}{(1-\eta')^{3}}\,d\eta'$$

    theorem CarnahanStarlingPrimitive.csIntegrandCancelEta (η' : ℝ) (hη'_pos : 0 < η') (hη'_lt : η' < 1) :
    (4 * η' - 2 * η' ^ 2) / ((1 - η') ^ 3 * η') = (4 - 2 * η') / (1 - η') ^ 3

    Pointwise simplification of the Carnahan-Starling integrand: cancelling η from numerator and denominator by factoring 4η - 2η² = η(4 - 2η). For η ∈ (0, 1): (4η - 2η²) / ((1 - η)³ · η) = (4 - 2η) / (1 - η)³. This is the core algebraic step from Eq.(5) to Eq.(6).
