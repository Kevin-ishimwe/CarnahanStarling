    Substitution of Packing Density into the CS Reference Term — Eq.(9) #

    $$\frac{A^{res}(ref)}{Nk_{B}T} = \frac{4\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{6}\rho m d^{3}\bigr) - 3\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{6}\rho m d^{3}\bigr)^{2}} {\bigl(1 - \tfrac{\pi}{6}\rho m d^{3}\bigr)^{2}}$$

    This is obtained by substituting $\eta = \frac{\pi}{6}\rho m d^{3}$ (Eq.8, packingDensityFn) into the Carnahan–Starling reference term $\frac{4\eta - 3\eta^{2}}{(1-\eta)^{2}}$ (Eq.7, cs_antideriv).

    The identity is a direct algebraic substitution (definitional equality after unfolding).

    The Carnahan–Starling reduced residual Helmholtz free energy after substituting the packing density definition η = (π/6)ρmd³, expressed directly in terms of number density ρ, chain length m, and segment diameter d. Eq.(9).
